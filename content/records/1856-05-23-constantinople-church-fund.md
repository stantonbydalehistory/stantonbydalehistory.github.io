**STANTON AND DALE ABBEY.**—Contributions were made in aid of Constantinople Church Fund by the congregations at Stanton Church of £1 6s. 2d., at Dale Abbey Chapel of 13s. 7d., and by the Stanton Men's Friendly Society of 1s. 11d.; in all, £2 4s. 8d.
