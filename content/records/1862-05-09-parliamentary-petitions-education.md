**PARLIAMENTARY PETITIONS.**

The following petitions were presented to the House of Commons, on Monday night:—

Against the Revised Code—from Diocesan College for schoolmistresses and elementary teachers, at Derby and neighbourhood, by Mr. Mundy; from the committee of the Derby Archidiaconal Board of Education, and managers of Stanton-by-dale and St. Paul's National Schools, Derbyshire, by Mr. Evans.
