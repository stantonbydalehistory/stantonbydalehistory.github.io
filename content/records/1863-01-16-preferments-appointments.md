**PREFERMENTS AND APPOINTMENTS.** — The following have been recently made :—Rev. J. C. Bradley, to the rectory of Sutton-under-Brails, Gloucestershire; Rev. G. E. Carwithen, M.A., to the chaplaincy of H. M. S. Indus. Guardship Steam Reserve, Devonport; Rev. G. R. Chell, to the vicarage of Kneesall, Notts; Rev. John Dait, to be domestic chaplain to the Bishop of Colombo; Rev. H. S. Eyre, M.A, to the vicarage of Newington, Sittingbourne; Rev. J. M. Freshfield, to the rectory of Stanton-by-Dale, Lichfield; Rev. H. Hall, to the incumbency of St. Paul's, Cambridge; Rev. W. Joy, to the vicarage of Shady Camps, Linton; Rev. F. J. H. Kendall, to the vicarage of Tulland, Cornwall; Rev. O. Owen, to the rectory of Gidley, Devonshire; Rev. C. W. Parker, to the rectory of Bulphan, Essex; Rev. J. Phelps. M.A., to the vicarage of Hatherleigh, Devon. Patrons, the Trustees: Rev. H. W. Pallen, M.A., to a minor canonry in Sarum Cathedral; Rev. John Wise, M.A., to the archdeaconry of Colombo; Rev. Fred. Augustus Howe Fitz Gerald, M.A., to the vicarage of Weybread, Suffolk.
