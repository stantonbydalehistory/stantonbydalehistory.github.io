**LET OFF.**

William Pawson, 254, Nottingham Road, Ilkeston, stated to be a very respectable man, was summoned on the evidence of P.c. Parfitt for being drunk and incapable at Stanton-by-Dale, on July 25th.

Defendant was let off on payment of the costs and no conviction was recorded.
