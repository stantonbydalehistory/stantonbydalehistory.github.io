*Jonathan Henshaw*, charged with stealing, at Dale Abbey, one plane, one saw, one chisel, and one file, the property of Stephen Sneap. Pleaded Guilty; 7 days' imprisonment.

Also with stealing, at Stanton by Dale, one plane, one brace, one spoke-shave, and one chisel, the property of T. Smith.
