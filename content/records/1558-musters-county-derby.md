<h3 style="text-align:center; margin: 0.6em 0 0.2em; font-weight:600; letter-spacing:0.04em;">
  MUSTERS FOR THE COUNTY OF DERBY, 1558.
</h3>

<p style="margin:0.6em 0;">
  Pleaseyth yt yor honolable Lordeshypps to be advertyssed That by vtue of the Quenees
  Majesties lres of Comiission unto us directed bearing date the xixth daye of Decembr
  we have taken the mustre and vewe of hir Graces subjects wtin the Countye of Darby of which
  doings we shuld have made certyfycat unto yo honable Lordeshypps before the last daye of
  February last past but by cause the Queneis Majesties lres were sent unto us upon such a soden
  to appoynt laborers forth of the Countye unto her Highneis Towne of Barwyck and also for lack
  of Justice some beying sick and some at London and beying further lett by thassyses we cold not
  before this tyme conveniently make certyfycat as knowyth God who ps̄erve yo good Lordeshypps
  long in honor. &nbsp; From Derby the xixth of Mch 1558
</p>

<div style="text-align:center; margin: 0.4em 0 0.8em;">
  Humbly yos to commande
</div>
