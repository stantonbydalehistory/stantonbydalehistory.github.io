**TRESPASSING IN PURSUIT OF GAME.**—William Raynor alias Stickland, was charged by Eli Winfield, gamekeeper to G. Crompton, Esq., Stanton-by-Dale, with trespassing in a field in the occupation of Mr. John Blunston, with a snap dog on the 9th inst.—Fined 20s. and 22s. 6d. costs, or in default to be committed to gaol for one month with hard labour.
