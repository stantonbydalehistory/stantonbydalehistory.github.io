**THE RECTORY, STANTON-BY-DALE.**

MESSRS. NEWBOLD and OLIVER (late MOODY and NEWBOLD), beg to announce that they are favoured by instructions from the Executors of the late Rev. JOHN GARTON HOWARD, to SELL by AUCTION, during the present Month, at the Rectory, Stanton-by-Dale, the excellent Dining, Drawing, and Bed-Room FURNITURE; a Small Library of Books, useful kitchen requisites, out-door effects, cows, pigs, and other valuable items.

Further particulars and days of Sale next week.
