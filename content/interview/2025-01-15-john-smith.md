## Introduction

John Smith has lived in Stanton-by-Dale for over 60 years and shares his memories of village life in the mid-20th century.

## Early Life

**Q: Can you tell us about growing up in the village?**

A: I was born here in 1945, just after the war ended. The village was quite different then - everyone knew everyone, and there were far more shops and businesses operating...

## The Village Community

**Q: What was the community like when you were young?**

A: Well, we had three pubs then, all of them busy. The Stanhope Arms was where the farmers and working men would gather...

## Changes Over Time

**Q: What are the biggest changes you've seen?**
