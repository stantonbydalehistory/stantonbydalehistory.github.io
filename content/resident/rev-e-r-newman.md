Rev. E. R. Newman served as curate of Stanton-by-Dale in 1883, serving under Rev. W. Fox, M.A., who was the rector and rural dean.

In January 1883, he attended the annual gathering of the congregation at the club room and proposed a vote of thanks to the rector for providing the entertainment.
