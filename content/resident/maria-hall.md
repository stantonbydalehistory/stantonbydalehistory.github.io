Maria Hall was born in Spondon, Derbyshire around 1789. In the 1861 census, she was recorded as a 72-year-old widow living with her son Henry Hall, daughter-in-law Tammy, and grandson Joseph in Stanton-by-Dale.

She died at Stanton-by-Dale on 27 January 1871, aged 82.
