Charles James Watts Radford (known as C. J. W. Radford) lived in retirement at Stanton-by-Dale for over 20 years. In his early days he was in business in Derby.

He died on June 2nd, 1923, aged 76 years, and was buried at Uttoxeter Road Cemetery in Derby.
