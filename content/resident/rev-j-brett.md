Rev. J. Brett served as curate of Stanton-by-Dale in 1889.

He chaired a Primrose League meeting at the Club-room in Stanton-by-Dale in February 1889, standing in for Mr. George Crompton of Stanton Hall and Rev. W. Fox (rector), who were both indisposed.
