Grove Farm in Stanton-by-Dale had an old open pit coal mine on its land. In March 1912, during a coal strike, Earl Stanhope gave permission to his cottage tenants in Stanton and Dale to mine coal from this pit. The seam was of superior quality and varied from two to three feet in thickness.
