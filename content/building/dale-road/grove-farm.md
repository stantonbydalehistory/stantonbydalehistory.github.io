Grove Farm was a farm in Stanton-by-Dale operated by Mr. Hollingworth in the 1870s.

In October 1877, the farm experienced a significant fire when a haystack of 40-50 tons caught fire due to overheating from insufficiently dried hay that had been gathered before it was thoroughly dry.
