The playing field in Stanton-by-Dale was gifted by the Stanton Ironworks Co., Ltd. in 1937 and equipped by the Stanton Parish Council. It was officially opened on May 14, 1937, by Mr. E. J. Fox, managing director of the Stanton Ironworks Co., Ltd.

The field provided recreation facilities for the village, including space for children's sports and community events.
